# Affiliate Disclosure Policy

## Principles

1. Travellers must be able to see when Travel Buddy may earn a commission.
2. Sponsored results must be labelled.
3. Ranking and “Why this deal?” explanations must remain independent of commission.
4. Demo/mock adapters must state that no live partnership is claimed.

## Required UI surfaces

- Super Deal Engine banner disclosure
- Trust centre affiliate relationship text
- Booking checklist / deep-link handoff disclosure
- Partner centre partner ID cards

## Prohibited claims

- “Cheapest available everywhere”
- Implied exclusive partnerships without approval
- Fabricated conversion or savings testimonials used as marketing proof

## Attribution

Clicks generate click IDs and campaign IDs. Conversion callbacks are recorded only when a partner posts explicit payloads — the product does not invent conversions.
