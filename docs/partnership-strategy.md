# Partnership Strategy

Travel Buddy aims to be a provider-neutral shopping layer that sends qualified travellers to approved booking partners with transparent totals and explainable rankings.

## Value for partners

- Intent-rich comparison traffic (whole-trip, not just airfare clicks)
- Clear affiliate disclosures and attribution windows
- Deep-link handoff with booking checklists (no in-app payment yet)
- Partner centre media kit + technical integration overview
- Onboarding status tracking per programme

## Value for travellers

- Fee-inclusive comparisons
- Multi-provider package views
- Trust centre (freshness, fees, self-transfer, sponsored labels)
- Preference-aware ranking without commission bias

## Commercial posture

- No public claims of partnerships before approval
- No fabricated conversion, traffic, or endorsement metrics
- Partner metrics in the Partner Centre remain `null` until production telemetry exists
- Live inventory requires credentials + approval; scraping is forbidden

## Near-term partner programmes (tracked, not live)

Booking.com Demand API, Expedia Rapid, Skyscanner affiliate/partner products, airline direct programmes, hotel groups, car-hire providers, activity marketplaces, insurance affiliates, eSIM partners.
